                          created by monnef and Tiartyos

This simple mod adds Jaffa Cakes (brownies, cream rolls, ice creams and more) into the game. I tried to make a not so easy craft chain - this mod has been mainly created to add extra content which could be used in factory designing and building. It also adds a bunch of [b]fruit trees[/b] that bear fruits (apple, cocoa, vanilla, lemon, orange, plum) and plants (coffee, strawberry, onion, etc).

[size=5][color=#008000]More information can be found on our wiki - [/color][url="http://jaffas.moxo.cz"]http://jaffas.moxo.cz[/url][/size]

[size=6]Recipes[/size]
[spoiler]Food
[spoiler][img]http://img11.imageshack.us/img11/7961/foodd.png[/img]
Sticks, buckets, tins, iron sword, kettles, knifes and mallets aren't consumed (but mallets and knifes take damage, kettle loses water - but can't be destroyed).
Omelette can be crafted using chopped tomato or pepper.[/spoiler]

Tools
[spoiler][img]http://img4.imageshack.us/img4/8499/toolsk.png[/img]
Wood and planks can be any type.
Kettle chain: <Empty Kettle> -> <Kettle with cold water> -> <Kettle with hot water>.[/spoiler]

Misc
[spoiler][img]http://img39.imageshack.us/img39/6610/misch.png[/img]
The bomb is crafted using simple jaffas and flavoured jaffas (explodes on redstone signal or when block is broken).
To get seeds for the new fruit trees (and plants) you have to craft a bag of seeds, then identify it by magnifier and unpack it (use item by right clicking it when holding it).


[img]http://www.mediafire.com/conv/859a9d0ecde7fee2f6354e33c29f01c80739c54139ffc1b2dac554ccaefaabc06g.jpg[/img]
GUI of a fridge. This machine is used to freeze ice-creams, sometimes generates ice and snowballs. Fridge needs fuel to operate (1 coal should burn for half hour). The inner temperature has to be below -5°C for the ice to be generated and food to be frozen (frozen food is marked by a snowflake in the top right corner). Tip: try organize fridge's inventory so the un-frozen food occupies as much slots as possible (but don't forget to leave a few slots for frozen food!) - it will increase the speed of freezing of your ice-cream [img]http://static.minecraftforum.net//public/style_emoticons/default/wink.png[/img].

[img]http://www.mediafire.com/conv/c0ea1f73113a29f72e3479fc676d96c8bd75319d3425c64cd6255bbc27d71af06g.jpg[/img]
The block at the center is the collector. When powered (using solid fuel or buildcraft energy) it picks fruits from our fruit trees and puts them into its small inventory.

[size=6]Notes[/size]
I recommend using some recipe mod - like [url="http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/909223-125132-smp-chickenbones-mods/"]Not Enough Items (NEI)[/url] or [url="http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/731133-132-craftguide-v152/"]CraftGuide[/url]. Jaffas mod is good for automatization because of large crafting chains. If you're planning to build your own factory remember to use [b]Automatic Crafting Table MkII[/b] from Immibiss's [url="http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/1001131-125132-immibiss-mods-smp-tubestuff-5001-core-5003-da-5001/"]TubeStuff[/url] for recipes that doesn't consume items (auto crafting table from BuildCraft3 destroys returned items!).
